ABOUT US

Captivate Your Spark

“We aspire to provide ethical and locally developed products while expressing individuality that is as unique as our customers. Our passion to ignite self-empowerment furthers our goal of building a more compassionate community.”
- Charmed JACO
Welcome to our website! Charmed is a student-run company formed as a part of the Junior Achievement Central Ontario program. Our company creates handcrafted pendants and keychains with unique and beautiful designs that evoke the charm within our customers. The materials used include metal bezels, non-toxic resin, and various decorative pieces. To ensure quality of our product and to provide the best customer experience, each charm passes through a rigorous quality control process prior to being packaged.

Charmed is also committed to making a difference as a proud partner of SickKids. For every pendent sold, Charmed will donate the profits from the sales to SickKids because we believe a brighter future starts today.
